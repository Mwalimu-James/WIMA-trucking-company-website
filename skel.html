About us.

Founded in the early 2000s by three visionary brothers—Wilson, Michael, and Arnold—WIMA Enterprises Limited has grown into a leading logistics and transportation company serving Sub-Saharan, East, and Central Africa. With a fleet of 300 trucks, WIMA Enterprises is committed to delivering efficient and reliable transportation solutions that meet the diverse needs of the region. From agriculture to industrialization, our broad range of services is designed to simplify logistics for businesses and individuals alike. We understand the unique challenges of transporting goods across the vast African landscape, and our mission is to make the process seamless, ensuring that companies and people can focus on what matters most. At WIMA Enterprises, we pride ourselves on our dedication to service excellence and our deep-rooted commitment to contributing to the growth and development of the communities we serve. Our aim is simple: to make logistics and transportation easier, faster, and more reliable for everyone across Sub-Saharan Africa. Let WIMA Enterprises Limited be your trusted partner in logistics, helping you move forward—wherever the journey takes you.
